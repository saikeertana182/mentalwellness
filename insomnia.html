Insomnia Symptoms:

  1. Difficulty falling asleep
  2. Waking up frequently during the night
  3. Trouble returning to sleep when awakened
  4. Unrefreshing sleep
  5. Daytime sleepiness or fatigue
  6. Irritability, depression, or anxiety
  7. Poor concentration and focus
  8. Increased errors or accidents
  9. Tension headaches
  10. Gastrointestinal symptoms

Insomnia Treatments:

  1. Establishing a regular sleep schedule
  2. Creating a relaxing bedtime routine
  3. Making your bedroom conducive to sleep (dark, quiet, and cool)
  4. Avoiding caffeine, nicotine, and alcohol close to bedtime
  5. Limiting exposure to screens (phones, tablets, computers) before bed
  6. Using relaxation techniques such as deep breathing exercises or meditation
  7. Considering cognitive-behavioral therapy for insomnia (CBT-I)
  8. Consulting a healthcare professional for further evaluation and treatment options
