Symptoms of Anxiety Disorder:

  1. Feelings of panic and fear
  2. Problems with sleeping (either difficulty in sleeping or excessive sleeping)
  3. Cold or sweaty hands
  4. Shortness of breath
  5. Chest pain
  6. Restlessness
  7. Dry mouth
  8. Numbness in hands and feet
  9. Dizziness
  10. Listening to music

Managing Symptoms:

  1. Reduce the intake of caffeine by reducing the consumption of coffee, tea, soda, energy drinks or chocolate.
  2. Go for a counselling session if you feel anxious for no apparent reason.
  3. Exercise regularly.
  4. Practice relaxation:
    • Deep breathing
    • Progressive muscle relaxation
    • Mindfulness meditation
  5. Drink plenty of water
  6. Avoid social media
  7. Talk about your anxiety with your loved ones (family or friends)
  8. Use scent: Lavender is well-known for its calming properties. Keep a small bottle of lavender essential oil on hand for the scent, for when you feel anxious thoughts brewing.
